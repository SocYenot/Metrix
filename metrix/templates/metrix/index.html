Welcome to Metrix

Metrix is an application designed for conducting sociometric research. With Metrix, your research can be conducted in a structured environment, where you can explore and understand group dynamics through scientifically grounded tools.

Manage your projects

Metrix allows you to easily create and track your sociometric studies. You can manage multiple research projects simultaneously, monitor their progress, and revisit completed studies at any time.

Participant and test management

The application provides tools for managing research participants and building sociometric tests from scratch. You can add personal details, configure group size, and create customized tests according to your needs.

Calculate sociometric indexes

Metrix automatically calculates key sociometric indexes. These metrics provide quantified insights into group structure and individual roles within it.

Detect sociometric relations

Let Metrix identify the stars and grey eminences for you! The application automatically detects sociometric structures, providing you with insight on your group's dynamics.
